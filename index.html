Our Paradise in Cebu, Philippines

Tagboanan Beach Resort, located on Northern Cebu's idyllic coastline, is designed for all travelers. Enjoy our luxurious amenities, captivating views, and the warmest Filipino hospitality for an unforgettable experience. From luxury suites to shared dorms, we offer accommodation for every traveler, complemented by awesome specialty coffee!

Sustainability in our hearts, not just a tag line

"Our pristine beach is a shared treasure — a place where local fishermen launch and return with the day's fresh catch. It's a living connection to our coastal community and a serene escape for our guests.

Wake early to witness their boats arrive with the sunrise, or unwind as they set off into the golden sunset. If you're lucky, you might even spot something fresh from the sea — perfect for our chef to transform into a personalized, delicious meal just for you."

Racquel Riveral

General Manager

One of the youngest General Managers in the Philippines believing in the harmony of tourism and local community.
